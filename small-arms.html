About our Small Arms

Welcome to our comprehensive collection of small arms, where advanced engineering meets unparalleled performance. Our small arms are crafted to excel in various combat scenarios, providing users with unmatched precision, reliability, and firepower. From cutting-edge assault rifles to high-performance sniper rifles, our products represent the forefront of modern firearm technology.

Our assault rifles, such as the ÖW ARX22, are designed to meet the rigorous demands of contemporary warfare. Featuring modular designs and advanced optics, these rifles offer exceptional accuracy and versatility. Whether in close-quarters battle or mid-range engagements, our rifles are engineered to deliver consistent and reliable performance.

In addition to assault rifles, our sniper rifles, including the ÖW SRX77, are renowned for their long-range accuracy and advanced targeting systems. These rifles are perfect for precision shooting, allowing operators to engage targets at extreme distances with confidence. The ÖW SRX77 is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure the highest levels of accuracy and effectiveness.

For those requiring sustained firepower, our light machine guns, such as the ÖW LMG88, are built for durability and mobility. These weapons are ideal for suppressive fire and prolonged engagements, providing users with the firepower needed to dominate the battlefield. The ÖW LMG88 combines a robust build with a lightweight design, making it an essential tool for modern combat.

Our submachine guns, including the ÖW MP-24X, are designed for close-quarters combat. With features like advanced recoil management and high accuracy, these weapons are perfect for engagements at short distances. The ÖW MP-24X offers a compact and efficient solution for tactical scenarios where maneuverability and precision are crucial.

Finally, the ÖW RP-45 represents the pinnacle of small arms design, offering exceptional power, accuracy, and reliability. This firearm features a proprietary custom caliber, making it a standout choice for those seeking superior performance. The ÖW RP-45 exemplifies our commitment to excellence, combining cutting-edge technology with meticulous craftsmanship.

Explore our range of small arms to find the perfect weapon for your needs. Each product is rigorously tested to ensure it meets our high standards, providing you with equipment you can trust in any situation. Our small arms collection represents the best in firearm technology, designed to deliver excellence in performance and reliability.
